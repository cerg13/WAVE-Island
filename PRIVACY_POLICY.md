# Privacy Policy for WAVE Island

**Effective Date:** December 27, 2024
**Last Updated:** December 27, 2024

## Introduction

WAVE Island ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application WAVE Island (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you register for an account, we may collect:
- Username
- Email address
- Phone number (optional, for iiko integration)
- Password (encrypted)

### Game Data

We collect information about your gameplay:
- Game progress (level, experience, achievements)
- In-game inventory and resources
- Planted crops and crafted recipes
- Spirit collection
- Purchase history (in-game)
- Settings and preferences

### Device Information

We automatically collect certain information:
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address
- App version
- Language preference

### Analytics Data

We use analytics to improve the game:
- Session duration
- Features used
- In-app events
- Crash reports
- Performance metrics

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

1. **Provide Game Services**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Save and sync your game progress
   - Enable multiplayer and social features

2. **iiko Integration**
   - Link your restaurant orders to game rewards
   - Verify order completion
   - Provide exclusive bonuses

3. **Improve the App**
   - Analyze gameplay patterns
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Balance game economy
   - Develop new features

4. **Communication**
   - Send push notifications (with your consent)
   - Respond to customer support inquiries
   - Send important updates about the game

5. **Legal Compliance**
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

### Service Providers

We work with third-party companies to provide:
- Cloud hosting (server infrastructure)
- Analytics services
- Push notification services
- Customer support tools

### Restaurant Partner (iiko)

If you link your phone number:
- We share your phone number with iiko system
- iiko shares your order information with us
- This enables real-time reward synchronization

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal process
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or illegal activity
- Protect user safety

### Business Transfers

In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale:
- Your data may be transferred
- We will notify you via email/app notification
- This Privacy Policy will continue to apply

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted password storage (bcrypt)
- JWT token authentication
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and logging

### Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Inactive accounts:** Deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted accounts:** Data erased within 30 days
- **Backups:** May persist for up to 90 days

### International Data Transfer

Your data may be stored on servers located in:
- European Union
- United States
- Russia

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update

You can:
- View your profile data in-app
- Update username and preferences
- Change email address
- Manage notification settings

### Delete Your Account

To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion

This will permanently erase:
- All game progress
- In-game purchases (non-refundable)
- Personal information
- Linked iiko data

### Opt-Out Rights

You can opt out of:
- **Push Notifications:** In device settings
- **Analytics:** In app settings (Settings > Privacy)
- **Personalized Ads:** In app settings (if applicable)
- **Marketing Emails:** Unsubscribe link in emails

### Data Portability

Request a copy of your data:
- Email support@wave-island.com
- We will provide data in JSON format
- Within 30 days of request

## Children's Privacy

WAVE Island is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in Europe).

We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover we have collected data from a child:
- We will delete it immediately
- Parents can contact us at support@wave-island.com
